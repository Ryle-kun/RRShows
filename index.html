What is Railroadiana?

Railroadiana = Railroad Collectables

In other words, any item ever used by a Railroad. Plus photographs, video tapes and books on Railroads.

Lanterns & Signals

Essential tools for railroad communication and safety, these lanterns and signals guided trains through the night and conveyed important messages to train crews.

Dining Car China & Silver

Elegant tableware used in railroad dining cars, showcasing the luxury and service that made rail travel a premium experience in the golden age of train travel.

Signs, Switch Keys & Locks

Essential operational items that ensured the safe and efficient movement of trains, these pieces represent the mechanical heart of railroad operations.

Timetables & Ticket Daters

Critical documents that organized the complex web of train schedules and passenger services, these items showcase the precision and planning behind railroad operations.

Hat Badges & Uniform Buttons

Symbols of authority and pride, these items represented the various roles and positions within the railroad hierarchy, from conductors to engineers.

Artwork

A large poster of train scenes stretches across the wall, each painting telling its own story of winter snows, glowing sunsets, and wandering tracks. Framed prints of a vibrant locomotive rest nearby, as if waiting for their next admirer.
